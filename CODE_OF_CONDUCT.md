# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and respectful community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on improving workflow quality.
- Welcome different experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private business data without permission.
- Abusive language or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or contributions that violate this code.
