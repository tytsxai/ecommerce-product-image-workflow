# Risk & Compliance

## Objectives

- Keep product true-to-source
- Ensure visual non-duplication
- Keep outputs commercially usable

## Major risks

- near-duplicate of supplier image
- product deformation by generation models
- unlicensed fonts/icons/background assets
- policy-sensitive claims in image text

## Mitigations

- maintain restricted elements list
- use commercially licensed asset whitelist
- enforce human QA before publishing
- keep minimal but consistent audit trail

> This repository is not legal advice.
